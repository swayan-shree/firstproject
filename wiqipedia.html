Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Born: July 7, 1981

Role: Wicket-keeper batsman

Nationality: Indian

Teams: India, Chennai Super Kings

Nickname: Captain Cool

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. Born in Ranchi, Jharkhand, he is renowned for his calm temperament and sharp cricketing acumen.

Career Highlights

  • Led India to victory in the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007.
  • Captain of the Indian team that won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
  • Won the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.
  • Known for his finishing ability and wicketkeeping skills.

Personal Life

Dhoni is married to Sakshi Singh Rawat and is a fitness enthusiast. Outside cricket, he is involved in various business ventures and owns sports teams.
